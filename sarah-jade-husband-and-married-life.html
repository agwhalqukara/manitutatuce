Pole dancer Sarah Jade Is A Proficient Artist

Jade is an instructor and one of the top pole dancers at the moment. Throughout the course of her more than ten-year career, she has competed on numerous reputable stages and maintained her status as a champion.

She has previously participated in competitions such as the “Florida Pole and Aerial Arts Showcase,” “USPDF Amateur Nationals,” and “Pole for a Purpose.”

In a same vein, she won the Florida Pole Fitness Championship’s pro division in 2012 and 2013.

Her other notable performances included those in the following events: “Vertitheal Desires,” “Miss Pole Dance America,” “Nude Nite Aerialist,” “Pole Show La Soloist,” “USPDF Battle for Armageddon,” and “Dance Filthy.”

In addition, she is an X-Pole Athlete and the 2016 Miss Pole Dancing America.

She also has her own business.

In addition to being a dancer, Sara Jade, the ex-spouse of Dave Bautista, is also an entrepreneur who has taught dance techniques. She is the creator of Buttercup Pole Dance, a prestigious pole and aerial dance coaching facility located in the Tampa Bay Area of Florida.

In addition, Jade represents Bad Kitty, one of the most prosperous custom shops in the US, as a brand ambassador.
